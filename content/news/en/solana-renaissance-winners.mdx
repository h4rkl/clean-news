## Grand Champion

[Ore](https://arena.colosseum.org/projects/explore/372), a new digital currency that enables anyone to mine using a novel proof-of-work algorithm on the Solana blockchain, received the Grand Prize of $50,000 USDC, along with passes to attend Breakpoint 2024 in Singapore, Sept. 19-21.

## University Award

The University Award, which recognizes excellence from a project led by university students, goes to [DeVolt](https://arena.colosseum.org/projects/explore/1085), receiving a $10,000 USDC prize for the project.

## Public Goods Award

The Public Goods Award, which recognizes an open-source project that benefits developers across the Solana ecosystem, goes to [Zircon](https://arena.colosseum.org/projects/explore/968), receiving a $10,000 USDC prize.

## Climate Award

The Climate Award, recognizing projects that are built with climate change and sustainability in mind, goes to [AquaSave](https://arena.colosseum.org/projects/explore/377), receiving a USDC prize of $10,000.
